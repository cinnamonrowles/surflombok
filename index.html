GETTING
To LoMBoK

From outside Indonesia: fly to Bali (DPS airport), then take a flight or a fast boat to Lombok.

AIRPLANE

Lombok's Airport (BIL) offers direct international flights from Kuala Lumpor, Singapore, Perth and domestic flights from Bali, Sumbawa, Surabbaya and Jakarta. Public transportation from the airport is lim- ited to a bus that goes to Sengeggigi and Mataram - you're best option is taking a cab ride to your final destination. It won't be more than $30USD and a few hours scenic drive.

Price: $$$ - $$$$
Speed: As fast as humanly possible

FAST BOAT*

Buy a fastboat ticket in Bali from your accomodation or any of the thousands of ad-hoc travel agencies on the street. Most will include transportation to the harbour in Bali and to your final destination in Lombok. Ticket prices are negotiable. Perpare to be crammed into a Perama shuttle bus to capacity with your luggage and other travelers on the included rides to and from the harbors.

Price: $$$ - $$$$
Speed: As fast as humanly possible

FERRY

Similar to the fast boat from Bali, except it leaves once a day, and the slow ride is longer and cheaper and more sea-sickness inducing. As with the fast boat, a crowded, Perama shuttle bus ride to and from the harbor is included in package prices. T ferry is usually empty enough for passangers to sleep through the ride on the bench seating.

Price: $$$ - $$$$
Speed: As fast as humanly possible

PUBLIC BUS*

Public buses run from Java and Sumatra to Lombok, sometimes on large ferry boats. These trips are slow, so very very slow, but cheap, also cheap.

Price: $$$ - $$$$
Speed: As fast as humanly possible

Favorite
sPots

Favorite waves in Lombok

GEROPAK BEACH

fun for errbody

Geropak has over 5 breaks for everyone from kids to more experienced surfers. It's easy to find, easy to rent boards, and easy to find a boat to take you out to the breaks. Recommended for beginners.

DESERT POINT

world class waves

What can be said that hasn't already been said about Desert Point. Voted the best left break in the world by blah blah magazine. 20 second barrels, and fun for spectators as well. Oh Desert point, need to find more to say here.

GILI TRAWANGAN

surf and party

Gili Trawangan may not have the best break in Lombok, but it by far has the best nightlife. Raging beach parties by night, and 3 - 8 foot waves over a gorgeous coral reed by day. The surf bar will get you set up.
